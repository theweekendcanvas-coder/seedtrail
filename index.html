THE BLUEPRINT SPEC

Behind the Design of Seed Trail

We designed Seed Trail with strict parameters to ensure money feels like something worth caring for, not something to be feared.

01 / REWARD CORE

Companion, not a banking client ledger

Seed Trail represents personal savings as a live, growing organism. When you set a goal, you literally plant a seed. Log your update on cycle days — and watch it sprout. Seeing it grow is the reward.

"No complex dashboards. No bank account integration required. Just you, your goal, and a plant that stays healthy when you do."
Sprout (10%) Seedling (50%) Flowering (100%)

Rather than displaying alarming numbers with strict graphs, Seed Trail reacts with beautiful stages of plant life to celebrate financial self-discipline.

Miss a month and your plant wilts. Log your update and it grows again. The feedback loop is immediate, visual, and deeply personal.

02 / COLOR SYSTEM

Warm amber — not banking blue

Every color decision was intentional. Deep amber communicates warmth and earth, not urgency. Gold is reserved exclusively for reward moments — goal completion, milestones, celebrations.

"We rejected every shade of blue and cold gray. Money should feel like something worth tending to."

The full Seed Trail palette:

Deep Amber
Gold
Terracotta
Warm Cream
Near Black

Natural greens are reserved for the plant itself — they are the organism, not the brand.

03 / FILIPINO CONTEXT

Built around how Filipinos actually earn

Most Filipinos earn on a kinsenas schedule — 15th and last day of the month. Seed Trail is the only savings app built around this cycle, not retrofitted for it.

"Reminders on the 15th: log your first half. Reminders on the 30th: add the second and save your Monthly Update. No calculator needed."
